What we do

Some people say life’s a movie. Others say it’s a game and that we’re in the matrix. Either way, why not have fun with it? That is what we do. We make games and applications to not only aid your life, but make it exciting.

Why

We have noticed that while modern technology has made things easier, it has also made other things harder. Yes, Instagram allows you to talk with someone on the other side of the globe. But how do you start an online conversation? And how do you keep a long distance relationship? It’s hard. Yes, Netflix makes it so easy to watch a multitude of movies, but too many decisions can elongate the time you spend picking a movie. Susu studios strives to fix the problems of the modern world by gamifying them.
